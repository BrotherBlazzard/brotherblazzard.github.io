Your capstone web design and development project is self-selected. Your proposal must be approved by the instructor and meet the minimum expectations of the course in terms of scope, relevance, and timing. You will need to work with the instructor to get your project approved. The purpose of this deliverable assignment is to formalize your project proposal in words. Treat your proposal like a blueprint and contract. The more detail you can provide now will help with the scope and quality of the final outcome of learning. 

Requirements

Submit a single page document or page URL that contains, at a minimum, this content:

Title

An effective title should clearly define the project scope.

Purpose

Define the purpose of the project addressing questions about what you propose to do and how you will do it. This project purpose description should be articulate in a succinct paragraph or two  as you summarize the intent and scope of the project.

Justification

In a separate section, justify your project proposal by relating it to your professional goals. The purpose of the capstone project is to provide an opportunity for students to meet the unique challenges of this degree program with its high correlation with the industry. Your formal degree program provides a foundation upon which you can build and expand as you add 'tools to your tool belt'.  The purpose of the capstone course is for you to selectively strengthen your academic experience leading to realistic, industry-based opportunities. Here are some things to keep in mind:

      • As much as you can relate this to your future goals and plans the better.
      • This 2 credit capstone course translates to a minimum of 50 hours. Your project must meet that minimum.
      • Recycling a project from another class is not permitted.
      • Enlisting the services of a MENTOR is critical. The mentor may be a faculty member or online instructor, but it is preferred that you work with a professional or related who has ties and insight into the particular area within the industry in which you are interested.  

Timetable

You will need to track all of the time you spend for this class. A projected timeline for your project execution, production and completion also is important.
